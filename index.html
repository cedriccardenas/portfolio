Change Healthcare - Optum

Remote System Administrator (Radiology PACS)
September 26, 2022 - Present

Full-Time

Change Healthcare's PACS system, which stands for Picture Archiving and Communication System, is a comprehensive digital solution used in the medical field to store, retrieve, manage, and distribute medical images such as X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and other diagnostic imaging procedures. PACS replaces traditional film-based methods with a more efficient and streamlined digital system. Healthcare providers can improve workflow efficiency, enhance diagnostic capabilities, reduce costs associated with film and physical storage, and ultimately contribute to better patient care.

The PACS system plays a critical role in modern healthcare, offering efficient and secure management of diagnostic images. Its digital nature allows for quick access to patient data, collaboration among healthcare professionals, and the integration of advanced technologies for accurate diagnoses.

  • Scripting
  • Active Directory

  • Documentation (SalesForce, KnowledgeBase, and Confluence)

As a Remote System Administrator, my responsibilities encompass a diverse set of skills and competencies. I am tasked with maintaining an in-depth understanding of the applications under my purview, ensuring a comprehensive knowledge of their functionalities and intricacies. This involves not only routine tasks but also a proactive approach to identifying and resolving potential issues.

I act as the intermediate contact for PACS Administrators, facilitating seamless communication and collaboration between the administrative team and various healthcare professionals. In this role, I play a crucial part in relaying information, addressing queries, and ensuring that operational processes are streamlined. My responsibility extends to providing valuable support to PACS Administrators, fostering a cohesive and efficient workflow within the team, and serving as a bridge to enhance overall communication effectiveness within the PACS system management framework.

It serves as a virtual marketplace where Robinsons' stores can showcase their offerings, and customers can conveniently explore, compare, and buy items from the comfort of their own devices.

Robinsons Land Corportation's eCommerce application was pivotal for their businesses, providing a platform that transcends geographical boundaries and operates 24/7, offering unparalleled convenience and accessibility to customers. Its significance lies in the ability to showcase a diverse range of products, adapting to evolving consumer preferences, and creating personalized shopping experiences that cater to the dynamic needs of a digital marketplace, ultimately fostering increased sales and customer satisfaction.

Skills:

  • User Interface Design
  • User Experience Design
  • Wireframing and Prototyping (Figma)
  • Graphic Design Tools (Photoshop and Premiere)
  • Information Architecture
  • Documentation (Confluence)

As a UI/UX designer, my role revolves around crafting digital experiences that seamlessly blend artistic creativity with user-focused functionality. I begin by immersing myself in user research to understand their needs, followed by the creation of intuitive wireframes and prototypes that serve as the foundation for visually compelling interfaces. My collaboration extends to working closely with cross-functional teams, including developers and product managers, ensuring the harmonious integration of design elements into the final product. Embracing an iterative design approach, I prioritize continuous user testing and feedback analysis, using insights to refine and enhance the user experience.

In my role as a QA, my primary responsibility revolves around conducting meticulous reviews of design deliverables within a project to ensure impeccable quality and consistency. I thoroughly assess user interfaces, graphics, and prototypes, identifying and addressing any deviations from established design standards. Through close collaboration with design and development teams, I provide valuable feedback, playing a critical role in maintaining a seamless and polished user experience throughout the project lifecycle.
